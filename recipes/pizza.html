Classic Pepperoni Pizza


In this reciepe we will be creating the classic pepperoni pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pizza baking sheet
  • Pizza Dough
  • Shredded Cheese (Preferebaly Motzarella)
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Pepperonis (Your choice for meat type)
  • Garlic Powder
  • Oregano
  • Pepper
  • Salt (If you choose)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F
  2. Grease/oil pizza baking sheet.
  3. Spread pizza dough on pizza baking sheet. The dough should be even all the way around. Thickness is to your liking.
  4. Open pizza sauce and spread on dough evenly with a spoon. Ensure to keep about half inch of area off the edge for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle garlic powder, oregano, and pepper over sauce.
  6. Add cheese to area that is covered in sauce. Amount depends on your liking.
  7. Add pepperonis on top of cheese. Amount to your liking.
  8. Place pizza in oven on middle rack for
  9. Remove pizza from oven. Slice into 6-8 slices.

Enjoy!
